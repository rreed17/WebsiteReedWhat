ReedWhat Collects...Marvel Legends

Marvel Legends during the Toybiz run I never removed them from the card so I never appreciated the sculpt. That collection has been sold and only a few remained in my collection. This Hasbro line has impressed me with their wide range of characters and build and sculpt work, I'm back in and this time every figure is coming out the box. Mostly comic versions is my liking but movie figures with the new head sculpts have caught my attentions. Hasbro Marvel Legends have dominated my collections.
