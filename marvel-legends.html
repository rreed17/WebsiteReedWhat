ReedWhat Collects...Marvel Legends

Marvel Legends collecting habit began during the 2000's era sponsered by the excellent ToyBiz 30 point of articulation waves. With that amount of poseable, I still stood fast and remained a MIP collector. So that collection was displayed on shelfs and pinned on walls of my first apartment, never to be removed for box or posed. As all MIP collectors, I sold most of them. Forward Fast to Hasbro take over of 6 inch scale figures, the line impressed me with their wide range of characters, body build and head sculpt. I'm back in and this time every figure is coming out the box. Mostly comic versions is my liking but movie figures with the new head sculpts have caught my attentions. Hasbro Marvel Legends have dominated my collections.
