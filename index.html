Welcome and Thank You for visiting ReedWhats' Digital Museum of Collecting! I'm ReedWhat, curator of this ever expanding, yet becoming quite niche, while maintaing focus on the collectables I truley love, collection of... Action Figures collecting has deep roots within me and I recall started with Marvel Super heroes Secret War Dr. Doom and Captain America with a follow up of a mixture of remco and mattle's motu lines, which may revel my preferance of toylines I collect as a adult. Physical Media includes comic related theme, such as DC animation and the juggernaut call the Marvel Cinematic Universe. And the first love that leaks into the other collections Comics. Started with Marvel Super Heroes Secret Wars full run for 15 bucks that I still own to this day. This journey is a lifestyle, that has been me for my whole life, enjoy and Peace.

Comics

The art of reading, enjoying and collecting comics was introduced to me by father. Shazam from the fourties to the sevenites and Marvel Spectacular starring Thor was the foundation for my core collecting habit. This is a small look into a lifetime of collecting comics. Entered with Classic Marvel, fell in love with the gimmick cover age, ground zero for Image, shy away during the over speculation era. Today the MCU brought me back, now I spend my days relearning Marvel 616 lore and also branching into outlaw and indie comics.
